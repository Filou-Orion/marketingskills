# Launch Timeline Templates

Ready-to-use timelines for different launch types, with week-by-week actions.

---

## New Product Launch (8-Week Timeline)

### Week 1-2: Foundation
- [ ] Define launch goals and success metrics
- [ ] Finalize positioning and messaging (use product-marketing-context)
- [ ] Identify launch tier (Major / Medium / Minor)
- [ ] Audit owned channels: email list size, blog traffic, social following
- [ ] List 10-20 borrowed channel opportunities (podcasts, newsletters, influencers)

### Week 3-4: Asset Creation
- [ ] Build or optimize landing page (use page-cro)
- [ ] Write launch blog post / announcement
- [ ] Create demo video or product walkthrough (60-90 seconds)
- [ ] Design social assets (screenshots, GIFs, comparison graphics)
- [ ] Draft launch email sequence (announcement + 2 follow-ups)
- [ ] Prepare Product Hunt listing (if applicable)
- [ ] Set up analytics and tracking (use analytics-tracking)

### Week 5-6: Pre-Launch Buzz
- [ ] Seed beta access to 20-50 early users
- [ ] Collect testimonials and early feedback
- [ ] Begin teasing on social channels (problems you solve, not features)
- [ ] Pitch guest posts, podcast appearances, newsletter features
- [ ] Activate affiliates/partners with launch assets
- [ ] Set up waitlist or early access signup

### Week 7: Launch Week
- [ ] Monday: Final team alignment, last asset checks
- [ ] Tuesday: Soft launch to email list / existing users
- [ ] Wednesday: Public launch day
  - [ ] Publish blog post
  - [ ] Send announcement email
  - [ ] Post on all social channels
  - [ ] Submit to Product Hunt (if applicable)
  - [ ] Activate in-app announcements
  - [ ] Engage with every comment and response
- [ ] Thursday-Friday: Follow-up engagement, respond to press/coverage

### Week 8: Post-Launch
- [ ] Send follow-up email to engaged-but-not-converted
- [ ] Publish customer success stories / early wins
- [ ] Update comparison and alternative pages (use competitor-alternatives)
- [ ] Run post-launch retro: what worked, what didn't
- [ ] Plan ongoing marketing cadence

## Feature Launch (4-Week Timeline)

### Week 1: Preparation
- [ ] Classify feature tier:
  - **Major**: New capability → Full campaign
  - **Medium**: Enhancement → Targeted announcement
  - **Minor**: Improvement → Changelog + in-app
- [ ] Draft messaging: What problem does this solve? Who benefits most?
- [ ] Identify target segment for announcement

### Week 2: Asset Creation
- [ ] Write announcement blog post or changelog entry
- [ ] Create feature demo GIF or short video
- [ ] Draft email for relevant user segment
- [ ] Design in-app announcement (banner, modal, or tooltip)
- [ ] Update help docs / knowledge base

### Week 3: Launch
- [ ] Publish blog post
- [ ] Send targeted email to relevant segment
- [ ] Activate in-app announcement
- [ ] Post on social channels
- [ ] Notify support team with talking points

### Week 4: Follow-Up
- [ ] Monitor adoption metrics
- [ ] Collect user feedback
- [ ] Publish use case or tutorial content
- [ ] Include in next company newsletter / roundup

## Product Hunt Launch (3-Week Timeline)

### Week 1: Preparation (T-14 to T-7)
- [ ] Create/claim Product Hunt maker profile
- [ ] Study top launches in your category (note: taglines, visuals, first comments)
- [ ] Write tagline (max 60 chars, benefit-focused)
- [ ] Create 6 gallery images (1280x800px):
  1. Hero shot with tagline
  2. Key feature highlight
  3. Before/after or comparison
  4. Social proof / testimonials
  5. Pricing or value prop
  6. Team or story
- [ ] Record 60-second demo video
- [ ] Write "first comment" (your story, why you built this, what makes it different)
- [ ] Prepare launch-day offer (discount, extended trial, lifetime deal)

### Week 2: Warm-Up (T-7 to T-1)
- [ ] Announce upcoming launch to email list ("We're launching on PH next Wednesday")
- [ ] DM 20-30 supporters asking them to engage on launch day (don't ask for upvotes)
- [ ] Schedule social posts for launch day
- [ ] Brief your team: everyone engages with comments
- [ ] Set up a "launch day" Slack channel or Discord thread for coordination
- [ ] Test all links in your PH listing

### Week 3: Launch + Follow-Up
**Launch day (Tuesday-Thursday, 12:01 AM PT):**
- [ ] Go live at 12:01 AM Pacific Time
- [ ] Post first comment immediately
- [ ] Share on all channels: "We're live on Product Hunt"
- [ ] Monitor and respond to every comment within 30 minutes
- [ ] Engage with other launches (reciprocity)
- [ ] Post midday update on social channels
- [ ] Thank supporters at end of day

**Days 2-7:**
- [ ] Follow up with everyone who commented
- [ ] Convert PH traffic: retarget visitors, special PH landing page
- [ ] Publish "What we learned from our PH launch" blog post
- [ ] Update listing with any badges won

## Beta / Early Access Launch (6-Week Timeline)

### Week 1-2: Recruit
- [ ] Build landing page with waitlist signup
- [ ] Define beta criteria: who is the ideal tester?
- [ ] Recruit 20-50 founding beta users via:
  - [ ] Direct outreach to existing contacts
  - [ ] Social posts describing the problem you solve
  - [ ] Niche community posts (only where allowed)
- [ ] Set expectations: what beta includes, what you need from them

### Week 3-4: Onboard + Iterate
- [ ] Onboard beta users in batches (5-10 at a time)
- [ ] Conduct 1-on-1 onboarding calls for first 10 users
- [ ] Set up feedback channel (Slack, Discord, or in-app)
- [ ] Weekly: review feedback, ship fixes, communicate changes
- [ ] Collect testimonials from satisfied beta users

### Week 5-6: Expand + Prepare
- [ ] Open beta to waitlist in batches
- [ ] Run product/market fit survey (use customer-research)
- [ ] Refine messaging based on beta feedback
- [ ] Gather case studies and social proof
- [ ] Begin planning full public launch (use 8-week template above)

## Announcement Size Guide

Use this to decide how much marketing effort each update deserves.

| Signal | Major Launch | Medium Launch | Minor Update |
|--------|-------------|---------------|--------------|
| **New capability** | Yes | No | No |
| **Affects all users** | Yes | Maybe | No |
| **Competitive advantage** | Yes | Maybe | No |
| **Requested by users** | Often | Sometimes | Rarely |
| **Revenue impact** | Direct | Indirect | None |
| **Channels** | All | Targeted | Changelog |
| **Email** | Full list | Relevant segment | None |
| **Blog post** | Dedicated | Section in roundup | No |
| **In-app** | Modal or banner | Tooltip or badge | Changelog entry |
| **Social** | Multi-post campaign | Single post | No |
| **PR/outreach** | Yes | Selective | No |

## Email Capture Popups

### Newsletter / Blog Subscription

**Template 1 — Value-focused:**
```
Headline: Get smarter about [topic] in 5 minutes
Subhead: Weekly insights on [specific benefit]. Join [X,000] [audience].
CTA: Send Me the Tips
Decline: No thanks, I know everything
```

**Template 2 — Curiosity hook:**
```
Headline: The one [topic] mistake costing you [result]
Subhead: We break down what works (and what doesn't) every [frequency].
CTA: Show Me What Works
Decline: I'm good
```

**Template 3 — Social proof:**
```
Headline: Join [X,000] [role] who read this
Subhead: [Specific benefit]. Free, every [frequency]. Unsubscribe anytime.
CTA: Subscribe Free
Decline: Maybe later
```

## Copy Writing Guidelines

### Headlines
- **Length:** 3-8 words ideal. Never more than 12.
- **Format:** Benefit or curiosity, not feature description
- **Test:** Would you stop scrolling for this?

### Subheadlines
- **Length:** 1-2 sentences max
- **Job:** Expand on the headline promise, set expectations
- **Include:** Specifics (numbers, frequency, format)

### CTA Buttons
- **First person** often wins: "Get My..." vs "Get Your..."
- **Specific** over generic: "Download the Checklist" vs "Submit"
- **Action + outcome**: "Start Saving Time" vs "Sign Up"
- **Length:** 2-5 words

### Decline Text
- **Respectful, not guilt-trippy**
- Good: "No thanks" / "Maybe later" / "I'm not interested"
- Bad: "No, I don't want to grow my business" (manipulative)
- **Consider:** "Remind me later" as middle option
